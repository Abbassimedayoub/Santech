Key Features

Company Management

Our Company Management module streamlines the complexities of handling multiple business relationships with pharmaceutical companies and suppliers. It allows users to store and manage extensive details such as contact information, payment terms, delivery schedules, and contract specifics. By centralizing this information, Santech makes it easier for users to access and update details, negotiate better terms based on historical performance data, and maintain compliance with contractual obligations. This leads to improved operational efficiency and enhanced partnerships.

Medicine Management

This feature tackles one of the core challenges in medical store operations: inventory management. Our advanced medicine management system not only tracks quantities and batch details but also integrates with supply chain operations to automatically adjust stock levels based on usage patterns and reorder triggers. It also includes alerts for expiring drugs, ensuring that no expired products are sold. Furthermore, it facilitates the quick retrieval of stock information, crucial during audits or inspections, thus promoting regulatory compliance and ensuring patient safety.

Customer Request Management

The Customer Request Management system is designed to offer a robust interface for handling all customer interactions and requests. From initial inquiry to order fulfillment, this system tracks each step meticulously, ensuring that all customer demands are met with high standards of service. The system also provides detailed analytics on customer behavior, enabling targeted marketing and promotional efforts. Additionally, it supports a feedback mechanism to continuously improve service delivery and customer satisfaction, ultimately fostering customer loyalty and repeat business.

Billing System

Our Billing System is tailored to facilitate smooth and accurate billing processes. It automatically generates invoices and bills based on customer transactions, integrating seamlessly with the inventory and order management systems to ensure that all charges are accurately captured. The system supports multiple payment methods and currencies, catering to a diverse clientele. It also includes features for issuing refunds or adjustments with full audit trails, ensuring transparency and accountability. Additionally, the system can generate financial reports that are essential for monitoring the financial health of the business, making it an invaluable tool for both daily operations and strategic planning.

About This Project

My journey with Santech started from my days as an operating room nurse, where I encountered daily challenges in managing medical supplies. The inefficiencies in tracking medication expiration, maintaining inventory levels, and managing patient records were not just cumbersome but also prone to errors that could affect patient care. Inspired to make a difference, I envisioned a system that would streamline these processes, ensuring safety and efficiency.

Santech is more than just a capstone project for my first year at Holberton School; it's a personal mission to resolve the logistical hurdles that healthcare professionals face every day. Developed with the latest technologies in Django and React.js, this application is designed to handle the complexities of medical store management comprehensively. By integrating these systems, Santech helps medical facilities manage their operations more effectively, ensuring that healthcare providers can focus more on patient care than on administrative tasks.
